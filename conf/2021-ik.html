Jam-Session

  • You can start a 1 hour jam session on Estuary clicking ‘Collaborate’.
  • If you do so, shout out the ‘esemble’ name, so that we can listen to your session.
  • If you also share the participant password that you created for your esemble, we can even join and interact in your jam session.
